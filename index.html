Zen Pattern Library - Andrea Ferreira

Theme: Night Sky

The theme for this project is night sky. At the top of the page we will have the starry sky as you scroll down you’ll see mountains, then trees then a lake that has a reflection of the night sky. The CSS Zen Garden title and intro message will be in the starry sky. “The Road to Enlightenment” and “So What is This About?” sections will be in the mountains. Participation and Benefits will be in the trees/ground. Requirements will be in the lake. The reflection of lights will be the same image as the one at the top of the page. The sections links HTML, CSS, CC, A11y, GH, “Select A Design”, “Archives”, and “Resources” will have plain complementary colors.
